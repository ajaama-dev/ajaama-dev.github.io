Maths

A-Levels

A Level Mathematics develops advanced problem-solving, analytical thinking, and logical reasoning skills. The course is typically divided into Pure Mathematics, Statistics, and Mechanics, covering topics such as algebra, calculus, trigonometry, probability, and forces. It challenges students to apply mathematical concepts to real-world situations and builds a strong foundation for degrees and careers in fields like engineering, finance, economics, technology, and the sciences.

2 Years

IT

B-Techs

BTEC Information Technology (IT) is a practical, career-focused qualification that develops technical skills and real-world knowledge in areas such as cybersecurity, software development, databases, networking, and digital systems. Unlike traditional exams-only courses, BTEC IT combines coursework, projects, and practical assessments to help students apply what they learn to realistic scenarios. It prepares learners for careers in technology or progression to university degrees and apprenticeships in computing, digital technology, and business IT.

2 years

Digital Production

T-Levels

This T Level is ideal for students interested in software development, coding, and digital design. It covers programming, project management, problem-solving, and creating digital solutions. Students complete a 45-day industry placement, gaining real experience in tech companies. It prepares learners for careers in software engineering, web development, or progression to university or higher apprenticeships.

2 years

Digital Support Technician

Level 3 apprenticeships

This apprenticeship focuses on IT support, troubleshooting, and maintaining digital systems. Apprentices work within a company’s IT department, helping resolve technical issues and manage networks. It’s ideal for those interested in IT support, infrastructure, or progressing into cybersecurity.

1-2 years
