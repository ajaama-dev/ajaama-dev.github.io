Accenture

Technology Apprenticeship

Accenture Technology Apprenticeships allow school leavers to start a career in tech while gaining a recognised qualification. Apprentices work on real client projects while studying towards a Level 6 Digital and Technology Solutions degree, developing skills in areas such as software development, cloud computing, data, and business analysis. The programme combines hands-on experience, structured learning, and mentorship, helping apprentices build practical skills and progress into full-time technology roles at Accenture.

3-4 Years

Accenture

Consulting Apprenticeship

Accenture Consulting Apprenticeships give school leavers the opportunity to start a career in business and management consulting while earning a salary and gaining a degree-level qualification. Apprentices work as part of project teams to help organisations solve complex business challenges, analysing data, improving business processes, and supporting change and transformation projects. Throughout the programme, apprentices develop skills in strategy, management, and data-driven decision making while receiving structured training, mentorship, and real client experience.

3-4 Years
